Pumpkin Pie Recipe

Description

Pumpkin pie is a warm, spiced dessert that is especially popular in the fall and during holidays like Thanksgiving. It has a smooth, creamy filling made from puréed pumpkin mixed with spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, and cloves. The filling sits in a flaky, golden crust that holds it all together. When baked, the pie has a rich orange-brown color and gives off a comforting, spicy aroma. The flavor is sweet but balanced by the spices, making it deliciously cozy and satisfying, especially when topped with whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin purée
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (9 inch) unbaked pie crust

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Whisk pumpkin, sweetened condensed milk, eggs, spices, and salt in medium bowl until smooth. Pour into crust.
  3. Bake 15 minutes.
  4. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) and continue baking 35 to 40 minutes or until knife inserted 1 inch from crust comes out clean.
  5. Cool. Garnish as desired. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator.
